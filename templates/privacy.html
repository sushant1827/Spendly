Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as pages visited and actions taken, used solely for debugging and improving the app.

We do not collect payment information, device identifiers, or precise location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expenses, summaries, and spending breakdowns.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, nor use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely immune to risk — you are encouraged to use a strong, unique password for your account.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated personal data and expense records are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Hosting providers — to run the application infrastructure.
  • Analytics tools — to understand aggregate usage patterns (no personally identifiable data is shared).

We do not integrate with social networks, advertising platforms, or data brokers. Any third-party service we use is bound by confidentiality obligations and is not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, please reach out through the in-app support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

For legal references, also see our Terms and Conditions.
